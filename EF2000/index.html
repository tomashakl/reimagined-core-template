The Original EF2000 (1995)

Released in 1995 by Digital Image Design and Ocean Software, EF2000 pushed PC flight sims with a dynamic campaign, real 3D terrain and a cinematic camera.

Why EF2000 Was Ahead of Its Time

  • Dynamic campaign engine
  • Real-time 3D terrain & weather
  • Cinematic camera & padlock views
  • Radio chatter and AI wingmen
  • Real-world theatres & mission variety
  • Time-of-day effects
  • Hardware-friendly performance
  • Flight model that felt alive

About this Fan Project

EF2000: Then & Now is a non‑commercial fan tribute. The goal is to place original DOS‑era scenes next to modern 2025 reinterpretations in a respectful, like‑for‑like way.

Image credits: original screenshots captured from the 1995 game; modern visuals are AI‑assisted artwork guided and curated by the project author.
